Get your first TuxSEO project up and running in just 5 minutes!

## Step 1: Create an Account

Visit [TuxSEO](https://tuxseo.com) and sign up for a free account.

## Step 2: Create Your First Project

1. Click on "New Project" in your dashboard
2. Enter your website URL
4. Click "Create"

## Step 3: Explore the UI

In the background we started a few jobs to populate all the relevant parts:

- Title Suggestions
- Keywords
- Competitors

## Step 4: Generate Your First Post

1. Navigate to the blog posts section
2. Click "Generate New Post"
3. Let TuxSEO create your content!

## Step 5: Automate the whole thing

For paid account you can give us your endpoint to submit content (in settings).

Once you give us that info we will start posting to that endpoint
regularly (you can configure this in settings).
